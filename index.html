Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and travel packages

How do I book a tour with Mr legend hoildays?

Booking a tour with Mr legend hoildays is simple! You can fill out our online form, call us directly, or send us an email with your requirements. Our team will get back to you within 24 hours with a customized itinerary and quote. Once you're satisfied with the plan, a 30% advance payment secures your booking.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI transfers, and cash payments at our office. For international clients, we also accept PayPal and wire transfers. All online payments are secured with industry-standard encryption.

Can I customize my tour package?

Absolutely! Customization is our specialty. We understand that every traveler has unique preferences and requirements. Our team will work with you to tailor every aspect of your journey including destinations, accommodations, transportation, activities, and meal plans to create your perfect travel experience.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy depends on the time of cancellation. Cancellations made 30+ days before departure receive a 90% refund. Cancellations 15-29 days before departure receive a 70% refund. Cancellations 7-14 days before departure receive a 50% refund. Cancellations less than 7 days before departure are non-refundable. Special packages or peak season bookings may have different policies.

Do you offer travel insurance?

While we don't directly provide travel insurance, we strongly recommend it for all travelers. We can suggest reliable travel insurance providers that offer comprehensive coverage for medical emergencies, trip cancellations, lost baggage, and other unforeseen circumstances during your journey.
